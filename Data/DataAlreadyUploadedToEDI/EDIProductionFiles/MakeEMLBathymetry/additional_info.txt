Author contributions

The WVWA oversaw and supported bathymetry collection in its three reservoirs. PAG led ADCP measurements and analysis in FCR. KAB led ADCP measurements and analysis in BVR and CCR with help from CCC and DWH. CCC and ASL coordinated data documentation and publication. Watershed delineations were led by DWH (CCR) and WMW (FCR + BVR).
