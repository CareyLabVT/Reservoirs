In 2018 and 2019, hypolimnetic oxygenation water quality management occurred in Falling Creek Reservoir. For a detailed description of the hypolimnetic oxygenation engineered system, see Gerling et al. (2014). This system was operated intermittently from April to November 2018 and again in 2019.
