In 2018, operation of hypolimnetic oxygenation was conducted at Falling Creek Reservoir. For a detailed description of the hypolimnetic oxygenation engineered system, see Gerling et al. (2014). This system operated intermittently from April to mid-September, 2018. Gerling, A.B., Browne, R.G., Gantzer, P.A., Mobley, M.H., Little, J.C., and C.C. Carey. 2014. First report of the successful operation of a side stream supersaturation hypolimnetic oxygenation system in a eutrophic, shallow reservoir.  Water Research. 67: 129-143. doi: 10.1016/j.watres.2014.09.002
