Authorship contribution statement: CCC developed and coordinated the ice monitoring dataset, led data analyses, and is the point person responsible for maintaining the dataset over time. ABP led the FCR meteorology dataset and FCR and BVR high-frequency sensor datasets needed for the ice cover classification, and winter fieldwork in 2023-2024. ADD automated the ice datastream and developed the maintenance log. FO contributed code for the determination of inverse stratification.
Acknowledgements: This data package has been iteratively updated over time and builds on the previous revisions. We thank the Western Virginia Water Authority for providing access to these sites and the Reservoir Group for contributing to fieldwork.
