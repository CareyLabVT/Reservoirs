Contributions to the 2023 revision:
CCC is the PI of the project and has overseen the FCR water quality monitoring program from 2013-present. ABP is the point person for the dataset and led sensor maintenance and data management. RQT initiated the new data workflow in 2023, which was implemented by RQT, VD, ABP, and ADD. RQT, ADD, and ABP wrote QAQC workflow scripts, and ABP and VD maintained sensor connectivity.
