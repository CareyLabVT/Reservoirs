Contributions to the 2025 revision:
CCC is the PI of the project and has overseen the FCR water quality monitoring program from 2013-present. ABP is the point person for the dataset and led sensor maintenance and data management. 

Acknowledgements: This data package has been iteratively updated over time and builds on the many historical contributions of co-authors listed in previous revisions. We in particular would like to thank Whitney Woelmer for developing the first QAQC function and Wentao Guo for developing an early version of the maintenance log code. We thank the Western Virginia Water Authority for providing access to these sites, the Reservoir Group for contributing to fieldwork, Vahid Daneshmand and Zehua Zhu for sensor connectivity, and Katie Hoffman, Bibek Kandel, and Molly Stroud for helpful reviews of this data package.
